Welcome to ESIS

Es Shemen International School (ESIS) is a private International School that uses the Accelerated Christian Education (A.C.E.) School of Tomorrow curriculum; a time tested Biblically based American curriculum that has been proven for over 50 years and in over 140 countries in 6 continents to emphasize Christ centered character training while delivering excellent academic achievement.

Our Vision, Mission, Motto, and Core Values

Learn about the guiding principles that shape our institution and define our purpose.

Vision Statement

To be a learning center of excellence for raising God-fearing, responsible, and highly skilled global citizens.

Mission Statement

To provide a unique and holistic education that is grounded in biblical principles, personal responsibility, creativity, and innovation in learning.

Motto

Diligence for Excellence

Core Values

Sobriety

We foster the life skills of sobriety, wisdom, discretion, and fairness.

Integrity

We promote consistency in adhering to biblical principles in word and deed.

Servanthood

We strive to serve God and others with humility, generosity, and compassion.

Student-Friendly Atmosphere

Our classrooms and environment are designed to be nurturing and supportive for students, ensuring they thrive.

Well-Stocked Library

Our library is equipped with books and resources across all academic levels, fostering a love for learning and discovery.

Forward-Thinking Education

Our curriculum, rooted in the Accelerated Christian Education framework, nurtures critical thinking and a global perspective.

Sufficient Classrooms

We have spacious classrooms to accommodate all grade levels, ensuring every student has a conducive space for learning.

Abundant Opportunities

Our students have various opportunities to excel academically and in extracurricular activities, encouraging all-round growth.

Innovative Thinking Education

We incorporate global best practices and the latest research to foster innovative thinking and problem-solving in our students.

Library Services

Our library is dedicated to supporting students' study, teaching, and research needs. We also use the Lira City Public Library to provide students with a wide range of resources. It houses a vast collection of resources, including archives, manuscripts, and photographic materials, aimed at fostering sustainable national and international development.
