Our Classes

What Our students Learn

ACE's approach to education is completely different from other curricula because it is Bible based and proves that indeed the "fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom". Its key pillars are; Goal setting, Individualized and Mastery-based, Biblical Values, Diagnostic Testing, Character Building,Academic Excellence, Critical Thinking Skills and Academic Prescription approach to learning. It provides a holistic education that touches spirit, souls and body.

ACE avoids the exam-based and teacher centered models of education which does't take care of the individual learning needs and gifting of each student, character development, personal responsibility and mastery of knowledge as opposed to cramming. ACE provides a pathway to university education globally. As of 2022, "Graduates from the ACE program are attending more than 1,400 colleges and universities globally with outstanding performance".

After school activities

After School Activities will be available at a fee. Activities include; board games/puzzles, football, bible study, book club/library visit, poetry, music, dance, story-telling, cookery and arts & crafts. Future activities may include swimming, golf, photography, filmmaking, computer lessons, and robotics. Regional and International Conventions: Students have the opportunity to participate, compete and perfect their talents in Regional Student Conventions(RSC) and at International Student Conventions(ISC). Parents pay directly for these events as well.

Admissions Process

Step 1: Visit ESIS Campus

We invite you to visit our beautiful campus at Plot 9 Dokolo Road, Lira City East Division. If you're in Uganda, come explore our facilities and meet our staff.

Step 2: Apply for Admission

ESIS has a rolling admission process, meaning students can enroll anytime. Ensure you submit a complete application for review. Call +256-777-230-537 to book an appointment.

Application Fees

Our application fees

To complete an application to ESIS, the application fee of UGX 150,000 per child (Registration, UGX 50,000, Application, UGX 50,000 Diagnostic Test UGX 50,000) needs to be deposited to the school's account. Payment may be made by bank transfer or direct deposit to any of the school bank accounts provided below, or though the school business office by cheque. Please ensure that the student’s family name is indicated when making a payment and if possible send a copy of the transfer or deposit slip to bursar@esis.ac.ug

Our placement of grades

Students are generally placed in grades based on the age of the child. Please note that we also consider previous school transcripts (where applicable), academic ability, social development, interviews and placement tests (where necessary) to determine grade placement at ESIS.
